# Security Considerations

Potential risks include:

---

## Oracle Manipulation

If the resolution oracle is compromised the market outcome may be incorrect.

---

## Liquidity Parameter Misconfiguration

Incorrect b values may cause:

- excessive volatility
- market stagnation

---

## Precision Errors

Fixed-point implementations must be carefully audited to avoid overflow or rounding issues.

---

## Simulation Validation

Simulation environments should be used to test system behavior before deployment.
