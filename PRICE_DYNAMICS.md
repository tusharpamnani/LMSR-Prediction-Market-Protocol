# Price Dynamics

In a binary market the LMSR price function simplifies to the logistic function.

P = 1 / (1 + exp(-(q1 − q0)/b))

## Price Impact

Buying shares increases price.

Price change depends on:

- number of shares purchased
- liquidity parameter b

## Target Price Trade

To move the price to P*:

Δq = b ln(P*/(1 − P*))

## Slippage

Average price paid:

(C(q + Δq) − C(q)) / Δq
